About Guidance AI

Designed to solve student career planning complexity through smart data metrics, skills training timeline links, and automated ATS analysis.

System Methodology & Logic

Our platform operates purely client-side via browser resources, calculating dynamic percentages that contrast multi-variable inputs against standard industry profiles:

  • Skill Competencies: Self-rated 1-5 sliders tracking engineering, UI design, copywriting, database metrics, and public presentations.
  • Academic Standing: Aggregating current grade standings to configure realistic milestones.
  • Interest Mapping: Interconnecting creative, technical, or research preferences with career databases.

Guidance AI was engineered under the core visual framework of SPACE_AI to support immediate, zero-latency feedback loops.

AI Calibration Engine

We evaluate the overlap between your current competencies and standard industry requirements. Gaps are highlighted dynamically, triggering structured course pathways.
